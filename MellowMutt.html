Mellow Mutt

OUR MISSION
To facilitate dog training using positive reinforcement in a way that is more convenient and provides better results for both dogs and their humans.

MY ROLE: UX Researcher, UX Designer, & UI designer

TOOLS: Adobe XD, InDesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop

What is Mellow Mutt?

Mellow Mutt is a mobile application for dog training anywhere at any time. It links to a FitBark collar attachment which monitors your dog’s activity, health, and behavior.

Mellow Mutt uses an AR camera to record and recognize your dog’s behavior while you are training to give both you and your dog the most positive training experience possible. These videos can also be easily sent to your vet or one of our professional dog trainers if you have any questions or concerns. You can follow step-by-step training instructions and unlock achievements to share with friends.

What is FitBark?

FitBark monitors your dog’s everyday activity and sleep and turns that data into deep, actionable health insights. It’s a new way to motivate you and your dog to be active, explain changes in behavior, and make better decisions with your vet about nutrition, mobility, anxiety, skin conditions and other health issues.

Mellow mutt is partnering with FitBark in order to monitor health, activity, and behavior. The mobile application will show your dog’s daily activity to help you understand your dog’s norms and detect when something is off.

Mellow Mutt uses the following A.I. Technology:


Machine learning & pattern recognition
- Identifies pain points
- Searches other databases for answers
- Personalization and recommendations

Real time emotion analytics
- Dog facial coding and emotion detection to assess dog’s personality, mood, and attitude

Next gen cloud robotics
- Stores user’s videos, saved articles, collar data, and more
- Saves data for vets and researchers
- Connect to data anytime, anywhere, regardless of data connection

Deep learning & neural networks
- Offers trick suggestions
- Predicts scheduling options
- Avoids sending notifications when user is busy

Natural language processing
- Learns how the user speaks to their dog
- Chatbot learns how the user speaks in order to effectively answer their questions and concerns

Chat-bot
- Answers user’s questions and concerns anytime, anywhere
- FAQ, training advice, and tech support
