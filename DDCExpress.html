Doggy Day Care Express


Stone Mountain Pet Lodge's sister company, Stone Mountain Pet Products, manufactures all of the doggy day care equipment, as well as the kennels, fences, agility, and play equipmentused by the facility.

In October, 2019, Stone Mountain Pet Products launched their new train-themed doggy day care equipment, which they debuted at SMPL's annual community Halloween Party. I illustrated this poster to advertise doggy day care and get people excited to send their dogs to play on the train equipment.
