Stone Mountain Pet Lodge Style Guide


Stone Mountain Pet Lodge (SMPL) is a family-owned pet care facility with two locations in Minnesota. Prior to working there, SMPL did not have any brand identity guide, so when I arrived, I had to continuously ask the two other people on the marketing team about what colors, typefaces, logos, so on and so forth.

With permission, I devised a plan for a style guide. I began by organizing the bits of information I had into logical order to give the company a consistent and profession feel.
