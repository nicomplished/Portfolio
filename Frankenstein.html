Illustration for the 200th Anniversary of Frankenstein


This project was a part of my Illustration and Design course at UW–Stout. The objective was to choose a scene from Mary Shelley's Frankenstein; or, The Modern Prometheus and use any medium to illustrate it; I used ink. I chose the scene where Frankenstein's Monster kills Dr. Frankenstein's fiancée, Elizabeth. To add drama, I used only black and white, and I actually made Elizabeth come off the page like a pop-up book.

TOOLS: Ink, brushes, colored pencil, & multiliner pens on bristol and foam core
